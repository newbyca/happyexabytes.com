Download Ambio

The most powerful white noise and ambience app available anywhere! Chill out with the many ambience mixes our awesome sound designers have created just for Ambio or mix sounds together exactly the way you like! All packaged in a sleek, easy-to-use interface.

+ Player Features +

  • Create your own ambience Mixes using any of Ambio's sounds
  • Create your own Playlists, play your favorite mixes together or create complex progressions
  • Use your own sounds and music from your SD card
  • Live mix editor
  • Gentle startup
  • Sleep Timer
  • Crossfader
  • Crossover
  • Plays when app is sent to background
  • Download hundreds of high quality audio samples produced just for Ambio

+ Content +

Ambio features the widest variety of audio samples you'll find anywhere and it's still growing! Our ambience library includes: White Noise, Pink Noise, Brown Noise, Ocean Waves, Wind, Rain, Thunder, Lightning, Meditation Sounds, Urban Ambience, Cat's Purr, Birds and other Wildlife, Chimes, Urban Ambience, Beats, Soothing Synthesizer Pads, ASMR, 8-bit, and much, much more!

+ Alarm Features +

  • Wake up to your favorite mix, playlist, music, alarm tone, ringtone, or notification
  • Gradual Wakeup
  • Solve math to dismiss
  • Alarm Test
  • Multiple Alarms
