Privacy Policy

As you use Ambio we want you to be clear on what information about you we use and how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What information we collect
  • Why we collect that information
  • How we use that information
  • The control you have over that information

We will keep this policy as simple as possible, but always feel free to ask us any questions here: support@happyexabytes.com

We do not collect personally identifiable information about you.

In other words, we do not collect any personal information such as your name, address, phone number or email address. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

We only collect anonymous information.

In order to provide a better user experience for you and the rest of the community we gather metrics on how you use our app. This information takes the form of aggregates such as how often you click a particular button or load a particular screen. We share this aggregate data with a trusted analytics company, Flurry, to help us understand how our app is being used. They use this data solely on our behalf. They do not share it except in aggregate form; no data is shared as to any individual user.

Flurry's full privacy policy can be found here: https://www.flurry.com/about-us/legal/privacy.html

Opt Out

If you prefer that your data not be collected, Ambio offers an 'Opt Out' feature that can be found on the main Settings screen.

If you would like to disable Flurry data collection for *all* apps on your device, Flurry offers their own end-user opt-out here: https://dev.flurry.com/secure/optOut.do

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will not reduce your rights under this Privacy Policy without your explicit consent. We will post any privacy policy changes publicly. If the changes are significant, we will post a prominent notice.
